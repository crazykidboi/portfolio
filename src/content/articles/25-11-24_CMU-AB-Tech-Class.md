# AB Tech Presentation at Carnegie Mellon University

The AB Tech program provides live production services for Carnegie Mellon University, I was asked to come in and give the team a presentation in live video. Specifically I was there to help them expand their knowledge and answer questions when it comes to recording and live streaming their events. Getting video in a live setting can be difficult, especially in theater where you wont even brightness and accurate color information captured in camera.
